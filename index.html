How I Became a Millionaire in My 20s

The Exact Steps I Followed to Become a Millionaire in Few Years

In 2005, I began with zero budget... and by 2013, I made my first million dollars.

Success in life is all about knowing how successful people forced their way through the obstacles they came across until they made it. I bet you know many success stories but the lack of details about those stories makes it so hard for you to know what exactly those people did in order to become successful.

I am pretty sure the longest success story you know can be written in few pages and such lack of detail is the main reason why you don't know exactly how you should proceed.

In this book, I wrote down my journey in full detail, starting from the time I was 15 years old and up 'till I became a millionaire so that you know exactly how to force your way through life and make your dreams come true.

The story includes the full details of the tens, if not hundreds, of obstacles I came across, the tens of failures I faced, the emotional shocks I have been through, the serious life decisions I took, the horrible mistakes I made, and the exact steps I followed to get out of each of those problems.

How Exactly Will This Book Help You

This book will tell you:

  1. What exactly you need to do in order to become successful in life.
  2. What are the fatal mistakes that delay success or even prevent it.
  3. How the road to success looks like and how you should prepare yourself for it.
  4. How to succeed in life fast by avoiding serious pitfalls.
  5. How to develop thick skin in the face of rejections and setbacks.
  6. How to have hope when it's really dark and how to believe in yourself.
  7. And ultimately, how to become successful in life.

What's The Difference Between This Book & "How I Did It"

While my book "How I Did It" explains in detail how you can start a successful online business, this book explains the steps you need to do in order to become successful in life.

The book "How I Did It" is a guide to creating an online business while this book is a guide for any person who wants to know how success works. "From 0 to 1 Million" is a must-have for any person who is serious about achieving success and/or happiness in life.
